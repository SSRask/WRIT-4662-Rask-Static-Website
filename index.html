What We Do

As you may have heard while applying to your position, we write the websites for all CLA departments. We collaborate with each other and with faculty to make department sites more usable for students. A lot of what we do involves:

  • Website audits
  • Meetings
  • Project management
  • Frequent, clear communication with department staff and faculty
  • Copyediting
  • Content development
  • Training

The Beginning

Prior to commencing this massive project of recreating all CLA department websites, we hired an outside consultant to conduct an analysis of our old sites. The consultant identified what all department websites had in common, and they figured out what pages supported the most traffic. With this information, we were able to create a common template for all department sites to follow. Meanwhile, this is all being migrated into a single content management system (CMS). With this, all sites appear connected and follow similar layouts, but there is still room for departments to flex their individuality. Working within a CMS also makes it easier for us to reach and work within any of the sites--faculty can help too!
